Pessoal, infelizmente nossos livros ainda não foram enviados. As coisas estão meio instáveis na White Wolf nos últimos tempos, e as compras coletivas internacionais serão enviadas somente após as compras convencionais. É desagradável, mas só nos resta aguardar e torcer para que não demore. Abaixo copio o último status da White Wolf que recebi, contendo estas informações:

"My apologies for the delay in getting this information to you but as you know things are a bit chaotic at CCP of late.  I wanted to let you know that the international bulk orders will get shipped out after all the normal orders.  That’s what I have been told by the powers that be.  When I was asked about this last week I was told most likely around the end of the month or early December.  I apologize for the delay in getting the books sent out but I did want to do my best to keep you informed.

Thanks for your continued patience with this.  CCP will get the books out to you it’s just taking a little longer than anyone initially realized."
